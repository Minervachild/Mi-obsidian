## 🎯 Outreach Rules

1. **Personalize the platform** — mention Wafeq/Zoho/Qoyod specifically (from scraping data)
2. **Always include demo link** — the video does the selling
3. **One CTA only** — "reply" or "watch demo", not both
4. **No attachments** — links only (avoid spam filters)
5. **Send between 9-11 AM Saudi time** — highest open rates
6. **Max 3 follow-ups** — Day 0, Day 3, Day 7, then stop
7. **Track everything** — opens, replies, demo views → feed to autoresearch
