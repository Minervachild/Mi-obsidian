## 💰 Skills Wishlist — Pricing Research

### Firecrawl (Web Scraper → Structured Data)
- **Free:** 500 one-time credits (1 credit = 1 page), no renewal, no CC required
- **Hobby:** $16/mo (3,000 credits)
- **Standard:** $83/mo (100,000 credits)
- **Growth:** $333/mo (500,000 credits)
- **Verdict:** Free tier is test-only (500 pages total, ever). Hobby at $16/mo is cheap enough for outreach scraping. [Hunter/Scout]
- **Recommendation:** ✅ Start with free to validate, then Hobby tier

### Supabase (Shared Agent Database / CRM)
- **Free:** 500 MB database, 1 GB file storage, 50K MAUs, unlimited API requests, 2 projects max, auto-pauses after 1 week idle
- **Pro:** $25/mo (8 GB database, 100 GB files, no pausing)
- **Verdict:** Free tier is solid for MVP/testing. Auto-pause is annoying but manageable. Pro at $25/mo is very reasonable for a shared CRM. [All agents]
- **Recommendation:** ✅ Start free immediately, upgrade when we have paying clients

### Resend (Email Outreach)
- **Free:** 3,000 emails/mo, 100/day limit, 1 domain, 1,000 marketing contacts
- **Pro:** $20/mo (50,000 emails, no daily limit)
- **Verdict:** Free tier = 100 emails/day is enough to start outreach. The 3K/mo cap is fine for Phase 1. [Hunter]
- **Recommendation:** ✅ Start free now — it covers early outreach easily

### Langfuse (Agent Output Tracking)
- **Cloud Free:** 50K units/mo, 30-day retention, 2 users
- **Self-Host:** Completely free, unlimited everything (MIT license, host on our infra)
- **Cloud Pro:** $29/mo (100K units) → $199/mo
- **Verdict:** Self-host is the move — zero cost, unlimited. Cloud free works for testing. [All agents]
- **Recommendation:** ✅ Self-host when ready, cloud free for now
