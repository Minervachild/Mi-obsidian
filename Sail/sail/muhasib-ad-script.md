# Muhasib Ad Demo — Script & Storyboard
*Format: 9:16 vertical, 30-40s, Arabic voiceover*

## Concept
"Stop wasting hours on invoices. Meet your AI accountant."

## Storyboard

| Scene # | Duration | Visual | Arabic Script | Notes |
|---------|----------|--------|---------------|-------|
| 1 | 3s | Close-up: frustrated business owner staring at pile of invoices/receipts on desk. Warm office lighting. | "كل يوم نفس القصة... فواتير، مصاريف، تقارير" | Pain point hook. UGC style (Kling 2.6) |
| 2 | 2s | Text on screen: "محاسب" with Sail logo. Quick zoom-in animation. | (no VO — sound effect: whoosh) | Brand reveal (Nano Banana Pro) |
| 3 | 4s | Screen capture: Muhasib chat — user types "سجل فاتورة ١١٥ ريال من مؤسسة الوفاء" → instant response with invoice table | "تكلم محاسبك بالعربي... يفهمك ويسجل فوراً" | Product demo — real screenshots |
| 4 | 4s | Screen capture: Muhasib showing invoice list with statuses (مرسلة، مسودة) | "فواتيرك، مصاريفك، تقاريرك... كلها بمكان واحد" | Feature showcase |
| 5 | 3s | Screen capture: Muhasib generating P&L report or balance sheet | "تبي تقرير؟ ثانية وحدة" | Speed/magic moment |
| 6 | 3s | Split: Before (messy desk/spreadsheets) → After (clean Muhasib chat) | "من الفوضى... للنظام" | Transformation |
| 7 | 4s | End screen: Muhasib logo + "جرّبه مجاناً" + QR code or link + Sail Solutions branding | "محاسب ذكي، يشتغل ٢٤/٧، بدون إجازات" | CTA — closing humor |

**Total: ~23-27s** (leaves room for pacing)

## Voiceover
- **Voice:** Confident Saudi male, professional but not corporate
- **Tone:** "I've been there" → "here's the fix" → "no brainer"
- **Tool:** Fish Audio (Arabic, Saudi dialect reference)
